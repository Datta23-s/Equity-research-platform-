Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the recommendations? +

Our model achieves 78% average accuracy on historical backtests across sectors. Recommendations are confidence-scored, with higher scores indicating stronger conviction. We also provide detailed rationale so you can make informed decisions.

What file formats do you support? +

We support PDF, Excel (.xlsx, .xls), CSV files, and images (PNG, JPG) of charts. We can handle scanned documents with OCR technology. For 10-Ks and 10-Qs, we can parse even complex footnotes and supplementary materials.

Can I integrate this with my existing workflow? +

Yes! Our Pro and Institution plans include API access. You can programmatically upload reports, retrieve analyses, and integrate results into your systems. We provide webhooks for async processing notifications.

How do you handle non-English documents? +

Our model supports 40+ languages including Chinese, Japanese, and European languages. Analysis is automatically translated to English. For non-English inputs, we maintain regional financial context and conventions.

What's your data source for market comparisons? +

We aggregate data from SEC EDGAR, Bloomberg, Reuters, and FactSet. Peer comparisons use industry-classified peers from Refinitiv. All data is real-time and updated daily at market close.
